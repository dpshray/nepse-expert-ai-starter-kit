## What is this?

NEPSE Expert is a Claude-compatible AI assistant designed to help beginner and intermediate investors understand Nepal's stock market safely and clearly.

It provides structured explanations about:

• NEPSE index  
• Banking sector  
• Hydropower sector  
• Microfinance sector  
• Insurance sector  
• IPO investing strategy  
• Right shares and bonus shares  
• Portfolio diversification  
• Risk management
