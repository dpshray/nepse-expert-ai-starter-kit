# Beginner Investor Mistakes in Nepal Stock Market

Avoid these common mistakes.

---

Investing based on rumors

Applying every IPO blindly

Ignoring sector strength

Over-investing in hydropower stocks

Lack of diversification

Following social media tips without research

Expecting quick profit

Selling too early during market correction

Buying during hype cycles

Ignoring long-term strategy

---

Smart investors focus on patience, diversification, and research.
